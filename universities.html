The University of Hong Kong (HKU)

Founded in 1911, HKU is the oldest tertiary institution in Hong Kong and one of Asia's top universities.

  • QS Global Ranking #17
  • Strong emphasis on research and internationalization
  • Top-ranked programs in medicine, law, and architecture
  • Located in Pok Fu Lam on Hong Kong Island
  • Local = 51%, International = 49%
  • Programs offers exchange programs, internships, summer schools, global experiences

Fun Facts

  • HKBU was established in 1956 as a Christian liberal arts university.
  • The Academy of Film is one of the top film schools in Asia.
  • HKBU is home to the largest Chinese Medicine Teaching and Research division in Hong Kong.
  • The school emphasizes Whole Person Education, combining academic learning with ethical and civic development.

The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)

Established in 1991, HKUST is known for science, engineering, and business education.

  • QS Global Ranking #47
  • Ranked among the top young universities in the world
  • Oceanfront campus located in Clear Water Bay
  • Home to the HKUST Business School and Innovation Hub
  • Local = 57%, International = 43%
  • Program offers student exchanges, research internships, startup supports

Fun Facts

  • HKUST was established in 1991 but rapidly rose in global rankings.
  • Known for its scenic campus overlooking the South China Sea.
  • HKUST is a leader in innovation, with numerous startup incubators and accelerator programs.
  • The university has strong ties with tech giants in Asia and Silicon Valley.

City University of Hong Kong (CityU)

CityU is a dynamic university with a strong focus on research, innovation, and professional education.

  • QS Global Ranking #62
  • Ranked among the top 100 universities globally
  • Known for computer science, engineering, and creative media
  • Located in Kowloon Tong
  • Local = 67%, International = 33%
  • Programs offers international exchanges, research programs, joint degrees

Fun Facts

  • CityU's School of Creative Media is one of the first in Asia and globally recognized.
  • Known for strong interdisciplinary research and industry collaboration.
  • Hosts the HK Tech 300 startup initiative, funding student entrepreneurship.
  • The campus features modern architecture and high-tech facilities.

The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)

CUHK is a comprehensive research university that blends tradition with modern education.

  • QS Global Ranking #36
  • Collegiate system with unique college identities
  • Known for medicine, business, and Chinese studies
  • Located in Sha Tin
  • Total students = 11,600 students
  • Programs offers study abroad, internships, global leadership programs

Fun Facts

  • CUHK has the largest campus in Hong Kong, spanning over 137 hectares of greenery.
  • It is the only university in Hong Kong with a collegiate system.
  • CUHK pioneered the first Chinese-language MBA program in the world.
  • The university emphasizes bilingual education (English and Chinese) in teaching and research.
